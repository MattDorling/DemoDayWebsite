Hardware

We aimed to make RoboGardener be reliable and adaptable to different types of garden envrionment. Three factors were crucial to achieve this goal: the design of the robot body, the design of the collection mechanism, and the electronic parts

Rugged Body

The body consists of 6 off road wheels and a waterproof plastic storage to operate in rough envrionment. The storage uses 2 actuators to dispose collected pine cones similar to a dump truck.

Quiet and Efficient

The spike wheels are mounted on the back of the robot. The wheels collect pine cones where RoboGardener goes without using any additional power. This mechanism will be noiseless and will minimize the risk of the robot harming any garden life by reducing contacting surface with the ground. It can also pick up multiple pine cones at a time.

Fast and Stable

Raspberry Pi 4B 4GB RAM with 1300Mbps dual band wifi adaptor will let RoboGardener have seamless object detection and long range connection between users and RoboGardener.

Clear Sight

Object detection is one of the main features of RoboGardener; thus, it cannot emphasize the importance of camera enough. Sony IMX219 8-megapixel sensor and the Exmor R back-illuminated sensor architecture can satisfy the needs with high-definition video.

Charge Ready

The base station with Raspberry Pi zero and a weight sensor will generate wireless connection among RoboGardener, users, and the base station to provide useful information to users such as battery status and pine cone gauge at the base station. RoboGardener will come back to the base station when its battery goes low automatically.

Budget

Overall price was estimated as we assumed to produce more than 100 units. Research has been done into publicly available robotic components, by comparing prices across popular electronics and robotics retailers.

Features Summary

RoboGardener has a wide range of unique features that set it apart from any other product in the gardening industry.

Privacy

We respect your privacy. The state-of-the-art computer vision system is run entirely offline. We guarantee that the camera feed never leaves the robot itself.

Connectivity

The base station connects to your home WiFi network to allow status updates and total control of the robot from the comfort of your home.

Auto Charging

You will never have to worry about remembering to charge your RoboGardener. It automatically mounts itself onto a built-in charging port on the base station.

Accessibility

We believe in including everyone. Our user interface is verified to meet the highest accessibility standards to ensure everyone is included.

User Interface

RoboGardener's bespoke user interface web app provides simple and intuitive control. It is compatible with any web-browser enabled device.

Scheduling

Easily schedule cleaning runs using the user interface. Your RoboGardener will automatically carry out a scheduled cleaning run when you are away at work or out of town.

Live Updates

RoboGardener's user interface will provide you with live updates about the battery level of the robot and information about what it is currently doing.

Navigation

RoboGardener will find its way around your garden using smart custom-designed algorithms. It can return back to the base station automatically.

Environmentally Friendly

We use fully recyclable packaging to deliver your RoboGardener. We also designed the robot with repairability in mind, reducing e-waste.
